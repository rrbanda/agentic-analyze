    The SivaClaw Story

    For their Series B, Lyzr used their own AI agent "SivaClaw" to run investor outreach. The agent responded to queries from 130+ investors, drafted dozens of investment memos, and tracked which slides investors lingered on. The round was 4x oversubscribed ($400M interest for $100M raise). This was a masterful "eat your own dogfood" marketing move.
